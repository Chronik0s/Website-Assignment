Biggest Events of the Year

Alberta Esports Expo

On February 18-19 of 2023, Edmonton held one of the biggest Esports events in Alberta since the pandemic. It saw over 500 entrants as well as over 2500 spectators! Artists and competitors alike saw a first in a long time, a live audience. The event was held at the Edmonton Expo Centre and was a huge success. There were many games played at the event, but the biggest one was Super Smash Bros Ultimate. The live stream reached a peak of 130 viewers and reached 2000 people total overall.

Lacombe Dome Anarchy Splatfest

Lacombe, a city of around 10k citizens ran a smash ultimate tournament over the final weekend of August. The event saw a total of 136 competitors

Biggest Upcoming Players

NootNoot (Ethan) is a smash ultimate player from here in Lethbridge. He is ranked at number 6856 in the world with Bowser. Outside of some very strong wins in Lethbridge and Medicine Hat, he has not had much success outside of Alberta. He is a very strong player and is looking to improve his results in the future. At GOML 2023, he made 129th place out of over 600 entrants. This result gave him the best results in Alberta out of all that attended.
